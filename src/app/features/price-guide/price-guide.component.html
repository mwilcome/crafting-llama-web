Pricing Guide

Understand how our custom embroidery pricing works. All prices are estimates—final quotes are personalized based on your choices.

How Pricing Works

Our prices consist of two main parts:

  • Design Fee: Covers the embroidery work, starting from the listed price. This may increase with complexity or custom options like special threads.
  • Base Item Cost: The cost of the item (e.g., shirt, hat, bib) we embroider on. You can provide your own or have us provide it for you (typically $10–$20 extra).

Submit your order for a free, no-obligation quote. Admins update examples via the dashboard for accuracy.

Factors That Affect Pricing

  • Thread choice (e.g., metallic +$5)
  • Item size and material
  • Quantity (discounts for multiples)
  • Shipping and sourcing fees
  • Custom fields (e.g., names, dates)
